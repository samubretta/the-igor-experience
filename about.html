WHAT IS THE IGOR EXPERIENCE?

The IGOR experience is a project developed by Samuele, an italian Communication student at the University of Bologna. The goal of this project is making you experience Tyler, The Creator's 2018 record "IGOR" in a whole new way.

The Idea of a "listening companion" for the record comes from a poor experience trying to study in depth the project, by using multiple websites.

That's the reason why in the page, you'll also find the Spotify player for the record. So that you can enjoy a flawless listening experience.

Don't esitate to contact us at theigorexperience@gmail.com to ask any question or give any advice!

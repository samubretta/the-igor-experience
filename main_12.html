Are We Still Friends? is the hopeful conclusion to this love story. It connects directly to the bridge in Gone Gone/Thank You: "I hate wasted potential, that shit crushes your spirit".

Tyler wants to still be friend with his former lover, because he recognise the importance of what they had and the potential of what they could have as friends.
